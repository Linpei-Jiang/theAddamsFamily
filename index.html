The Addams Family

About The Addams Family

The story follows Wednesday Addams, who falls in love with a “normal” boy named Lucas Beineke. She invites him and his very traditional parents over for dinner, which quickly turns awkward as the strange and spooky Addams family tries (and fails) to act normal. At the same time, Gomez is stuck keeping Wednesday’s relationship a secret from Morticia, which puts a strain on their marriage. During dinner, a game forces everyone to tell the truth, and secrets start coming out.
