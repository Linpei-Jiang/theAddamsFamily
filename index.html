Forest Hills High School

Information From FHHS

Forest Hills High School endeavors to provide opportunities for engagement for its learners and community members both inside the classroom through meaningful materials and outside the classroom through interesting programs. These experiences will provide our learners and the community with the supportive and enriching environment needed to grow, achieve and succeed. We encourage high expectations and challenge our students academically to ensure they become lifelong learners and caring citizens.
