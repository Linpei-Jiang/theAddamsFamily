Welcome!

Mr. Koch dedicated 24 years of his life to his students through the Music Department, Women's Chorus, Concert Choir and Play Pro. He gave so much of himself to Forest Hills High School's Music Program and to the children he cared for so deeply. In lieu of flowers, the Koch family wishes for donations to be made in his memory to Forest Hills High School and its music programs supporting the music academies, chorus programs, Play Pro and the student experiences he was so passionate about as well as a scholarship in his name so that his legacy can continue to support FHHS students for years to come. We invite families to make a donation via check to Forest Hills High School Parent Association or via Zelle to pafhhs@gmail.com.

- The Koch Family

About The Addams Family

The story follows Wednesday Addams, a young girl who falls in love with a “normal” boy named Lucas Beineke. She invites him and his very traditional parents over for dinner, which quickly turns awkward as the strange and spooky Addams family tries (and fails) to act normal. During dinner, a game forces everyone to tell the truth, and secrets start coming out.

At the same time, Wednesday’s father, Gomez, is stuck keeping his daughter’s relationship a secret from his wife, Morticia, which ultimately puts a strain on their marriage. Balancing family and relationship norms with humor and music creates this humouros and eccentric musical.
